Game Rules

Objective

Get a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer without going over.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value
  • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10
  • Ace can be worth 1 or 11 (whichever is better for your hand)

Game Actions

  • Hit: Take another card
  • Stand: Keep your current hand
  • Double: Double your bet and take exactly one more card

Winning

  • If your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's, you win 1:1
  • If you get Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card), you win 3:2
  • If you go over 21, you bust and lose your bet
  • If the dealer busts, you win
