Poker Rules

Objective

Win chips by having the best hand or by making other players fold.

Hand Rankings

  1. Royal Flush: A, K, Q, J, 10 of the same suit
  2. Straight Flush: Five consecutive cards of the same suit
  3. Four of a Kind: Four cards of the same rank
  4. Full House: Three of a kind plus a pair
  5. Flush: Five cards of the same suit
  6. Straight: Five consecutive cards
  7. Three of a Kind: Three cards of the same rank
  8. Two Pair: Two different pairs
  9. One Pair: Two cards of the same rank
  10. High Card: Highest card when no other hand is made

Gameplay

  1. Place your initial bet
  2. Receive your two hole cards
  3. First betting round (Call, Raise, or Fold)
  4. Flop: Three community cards are dealt
  5. Second betting round
  6. Turn: Fourth community card
  7. Third betting round
  8. River: Fifth community card
  9. Final betting round
  10. Showdown: Best hand wins the pot

Betting Options

  • Fold: Give up your hand and lose your bet
  • Call: Match the current bet
  • Raise: Increase the current bet
